AS3Dmod demo: Skew modifier

No flash player

  • Mouse move - change Skew.offset
  • UP/DOWN - increase, decrease Skew.force
  • LEFT/RIGHT - increase, decrease Skew.falloff
  • B - toggle Skew.inverseFalloff
  • X,Y,Z - change Skew.skewAxis
  • A - toggle Skew.swapAxes
  • S - toggle Skew.oneSide
  • Q,W - decrease, increase Skew.power
  • L,R,N - set Skew.constraint to LEFT/RIGHT/NONE respectively
Make sure the Flash piece is in focus (click on the animation).
Engine: Papervision3d
